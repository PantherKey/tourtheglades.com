About Us

Tour The Glades operates with a simple goal:

Helping you navigate the unknown habitat of the Everglades and 10,000 Islands.

We strive to be a full service host for your visit to the Everglades area. We are unique in that we operate as both an adventure outfitter and concierge; we guide you through both the swamps and neighboring destinations to enhance your Everglades experience! Our tours are geared towards the ecology and wildlife while the local historical perspective will be explored as well. We’re a small family business and give family discounts to make it affordable for everyone. We supply everything you’ll need for your adventure which makes planning and preparing easy.We only do Private Tours with Native Guides. We find that the smaller the group and the less noise the more wildlife you’ll encounter. This allows you to move along more quietly and enhances your overall experience. When you set out with a mixed group of people it’s just not the same as going on your own. When choosing a guide (no mater where you’re visiting) you’ll want to go with a Native. Someone born and raised in the area with a true passion for what they’re doing will make the difference in your trip. Our Private Tour / Native Guide approach allows our guests to experience the Everglades in a very unique and special way……we’re confident you’ll agree.
