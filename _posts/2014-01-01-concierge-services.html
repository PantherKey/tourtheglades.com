Our Favorites

Everglades City is a small community with less then 600 very proud permanent residents. The hotels and restaurants here are all family owned and operated which makes them unique. Each of the restaurants have their own specialties you'll want to try during your visit. This is a Foodies Paradise! There are a few good hotels in town and a haunted one as well. I've listed my favorites below (email me if you're interested in the haunted one). I've also included a few things you can do on your own if you like.

 

Local Accommodations

  • Ivey House B&B
  • Everglades City Motel
  • Port of the Islands

Good Food

  • Camellia Street Grill *BEST LUNCH - Blackened Fish Tacos and Terri's Fried Green Tomatoes
  • Sweet Mayberry's Cafe *BEST ALL AROUND - Greek Wrap - Key Lime Cake and Joane's fresh Coffee
  • Havana Cafe *BEST DINNER - This is a restaurant that feels like a home. Try Carlos' Famous Shrimp Plate
  • Grimm's Stone Crab *BEST STONE CRAB - If you're serious about Stone Crab this is your place...these are right off the boat! Ask Justin for the family mustard sauce as well.
  • Island Cafe *BEST BREAKFAST - Fish & Grits or Carol's Biscuits & Gravy
  • Oyster House *BEST LOCAL PUB - Anything Cold with Bobby's Fried Gator will work
  • Triad Seafood *BEST BBQ - Don't leave town without trying Orlo's BBQ Ribs
  • Rod & Gun Club *BEST VIEW - Relax and watch the boats come in during your meal. Taylor will make sure you're taken care of.
  • City Seafood *BEST BOATING STOP - Dock Space & Good Food...Candice will hook you up.
  • Joanie's Blue Crab Cafe *BEST ROADSIDE STOP - This Place Is Legendary! Crab Cakes or the Swamp Combo....ask Lisa to show you their resident Alligator out back
  • Anglers Cove at Port of the Islands *BEST MUSIC - In between Everglades City & Marco Island...ask Joyce for the Tuna Poke

Hotels, Little Roadside Stops & Good Restaurants along the Loop

Many of our guests are visiting multiple places in Florida during their stay, this is known as “The Loop”. The Loop consists of Orlando, Miami & The Keys, The Everglades and one of the beautiful West Coast destinations. Sanibel & Captiva Islands, St. Petersburg, Sarasota, etc. Even if you’re not doing the entire loop you’ll want to check out some of our Favorite Places to stay & things to do along the way.

Tampa / St Petersburg Area

  • See live Mermaids at Weiki Watchee Springs
  • Tarpon Springs Sponge Docks
  • The hidden beach town of Pass-a-Grille Beach
  • Historic Plantation Inn - Crystal River
  • Swim with Manatees in Paradise

Marathon / Islamorada, Florida Keys

  • Feed the giant Tarpon at Robbies Resort
  • Visit the sea turtles at The Turtle Hospital
  • Florida Keys Diving Museum
  • Hawks Cay Resort - Marathon
  • Catch Lobster for dinner with Kevin at Florida Keys Reel Adventures
  • This is the BEST Southermost Sunset by far!

Cape Canaveral / Orlando Area

  • Dixie Crossroads for dinner
  • Pick your own Oranges at the Showcase of Citrus
  • Alligators & Rockets at Merritt Island National Wildlife Refuge
  • Meet a Moon Walker at the Astronaut Hall of Fame
  • Royal Mansions Resort - Orlando
  • Check the launch schudle at Kennedy Space Center

Sanibel & Captiva Islands

Ft. Lauderdale / Miami Beach Area
