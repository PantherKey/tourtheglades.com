Fossil Expedition

Some of Florida’s beaches and ancient rivers are full of sharks teeth and various other fossil material from the Miocene and Pleistocene epic. There are no dinosaur bones here because Florida was completely under water during their time. Some of the creatures that did inhabit Florida’s early landscape include mastodon, mammoth, giant sloth, jaguar, dire wolf, saber cat, camel and giant armadillo. The surrounding waters were home to the giant Magladon and Great White shark as well as massive crocodile and alligator. Teeth and fossils remains from these great beasts and many others can be found throughout the state.

With a little research and a trained eye you can find the areas where these rich, concentrated fossil deposits lie. We’ll travel to the locations by kayak or small motorboat and sift through millions of years of Florida history. You’ll go back home with a new appreciation of our past and some nice pieces to display proudly.
