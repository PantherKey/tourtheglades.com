Journey To The 10,000 Islands

Venture down an ancient river through the mangrove tunnels and sawgrass prairie to the beautiful 10,000 Islands. Known as the Amazon of North America, you’ll glide under the canopy of our country’s only subtropical wilderness all the way to the mysterious Fakahatchee Island. During our exploration of the island you’ll see remanence of the extinct Calusa Indians as well as the early settlers of the 1800′s. From the Island you’ll enjoy a leisurely motorboat tour through the 10,000 Islands and back to Everglades City.

$250 per person

This trip is not recommended for children under 13 years of age.
